Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common travel questions. Our AI learns from these to provide better assistance.

How do I cancel my booking?

You can cancel your booking up to 2 hours before departure through the AI assistant or your dashboard. Refund policies vary by operator.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept bKash, Nagad, Rocket, credit/debit cards, and bank transfers. All payments are secure and encrypted.

Can I track my bus in real-time?

Yes! All our partner buses have GPS tracking. You'll receive live updates about location, delays, and estimated arrival times.

What if my bus is delayed?

Our AI assistant will notify you immediately of any delays and suggest alternative options or compensation based on operator policies.

Is the AI assistant available 24/7?

Yes, our AI assistant is available round the clock to help with bookings, queries, and journey support in both Bengali and English.

How accurate are the recommendations?

Our AI uses your travel history, preferences, and real-time data to provide highly personalized recommendations with 95% satisfaction rate.
