Travel Tips & Cultural Etiquette

Essential guidance for respectful and enriching travel experiences across Bangladesh.

Dress Respectfully

Wear modest clothing, especially when visiting religious sites. Cover shoulders and knees.

Best Travel Seasons

October to March offers pleasant weather. Avoid monsoon season (June-September) for outdoor activities.

Local Language

Learn basic Bengali phrases. "Dhonnobad" (thank you) and "Assalamu Alaikum" (greeting) are appreciated.

Food Safety

Try street food from busy stalls, drink bottled water, and carry hand sanitizer for hygiene.
